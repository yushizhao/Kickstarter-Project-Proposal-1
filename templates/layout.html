Background

Kickstarter maintains a global crowdfunding platform focused on creativity such as films, music, stage shows, comics, journalism, video games, technology and food. On Kickstarter, people can present their ideas as projects in order to get fund from broad audiences. Project creators choose a deadline and a minimum funding goal. If the goal is not met by the deadline, no funds are collected (a kind of assurance contract).
